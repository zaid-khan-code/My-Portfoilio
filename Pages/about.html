About Me

Aspiring web developer and computer science student (Fresher) with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, Firebase, and Firestore. Passionate about building modern, responsive, and creative web experiences.

My Journey

My journey in technology began during my 9th and 10th grade years when a family member suggested I try coding. At first, I thought it would be easy and maybe even a bit boring, but once I started learning from YouTube tutorials, I discovered how fascinating it truly was. What started as a simple suggestion from a family member has now become a true passion for coding and building digital experiences.

Throughout my learning journey, I’ve had the opportunity to work with modern and cutting-edge technologies including Tailwind CSS, Firebase, Firestore, JavaScript, and GitHub. I strongly believe in the power of technology to create impact, transform ideas into reality, and improve lives — a belief that drives my ongoing commitment to continuous learning and innovation.

When there is a new technology, you’ll find me learning it and building projects around it. I strongly believe in sharing knowledge, because when I had none, I was truly eager and desperate to find it.
